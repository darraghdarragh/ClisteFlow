ClisteFlow helps Irish businesses work smarter through AI automation, connected systems, workflow design, and modern digital experiences.

Our Story

From websites to intelligent workflow systems

ClisteFlow was founded by Darragh Morrissey to help businesses improve how they operate, communicate, and grow through smarter digital systems.

What began with modern website design has grown into a broader focus on AI-powered workflows, automation systems, document generation, and connected business tools that reduce manual work and improve delivery.

Our Mission

Practical automation that creates real value

Our mission is to help businesses work smarter, not harder, using AI, automation, and modern technology to improve operations, customer experience, and long-term scalability.

We focus on clean execution, premium design, and practical systems that make everyday business processes faster, clearer, and easier to manage.

What We Focus On

Automation built around real business problems

ClisteFlow focuses on systems that save time, reduce admin, improve consistency, and help businesses deliver work more efficiently.

AI Automation

Workflow Systems
